About GreenLeaf Retail

A fictional 8,000-employee Indian omnichannel retailer spanning Metro to Tier-3 India. Payroll is already mature at Level 4. Analytics, performance management, and workforce planning sit at Levels 1–2: spreadsheets, paper files, and gut decisions. A new CHRO has secured a ₹6 crore, six-quarter transformation mandate from a skeptical Board. That mandate is yours.
