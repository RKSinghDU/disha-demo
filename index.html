The mission

You are the Digital HR Transformation Office (DHTO) at GreenLeaf Retail — a fictional 8,000-employee Indian omnichannel retailer. Over six compressed quarters, carry GreenLeaf from a paper-heavy, fragmented HR function to one that is digital, analytical, equitable, and Board-ready.

"Disha" (दिशा) means direction. You are setting GreenLeaf's direction for the digital decade.

The three scarce currencies

₹ Budget

₹6 crore total across all six rounds. Overspending is penalised. Under-investing that starves adoption is also penalised.

Change Capital

Starts at 100. Organisational trust and political goodwill. Persists round-to-round. Falls below 30 and resistance intensifies.

Analyst Hours

60 hours per round, resets each round. Deep analysis costs more hours but unlocks richer evidence.
